Meet Omotayo Abdulahi

I am young Graphic Designer and sometimes also a Photographer, with over 6 years of experience in the design field, helping brands and businesses to promote themselves effectively. I have worked in an advertising agency as Assistant Creative designer and a magazine company as the head of creatives and strategy. My expertise includes all aspect of design from Logos, Business cards, print/online adverts, web banners, posters, flyers, magazines and full branding service. I am an expert in Adobe Creative Suite (Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop), and proficient in the use of Corel Draw package.

My name is Omotayo Abdulahi. I'm passionate about Graphics, Branding, and Photography.
