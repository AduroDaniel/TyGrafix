I am young Graphic Designer and sometimes also a Photographer, with over 6 years of experience in the design field, helping brands and businesses to promote themselves effectively. My expertise includes all aspect of design from Logos, Business cards, print/online adverts, web banners, posters, flyers, magazines and full branding service.
